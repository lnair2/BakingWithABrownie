Info

Spicy Tofu is a side dish that Amma usually serves with rice, but tastes just as good with chapati or in a salad. It takes no time at all, and you're getting your protein in for the day.
Time
20 minutes
Servings
4
Spice Level
Medium

Ingredients

1 container
Firm Tofu
1 tb
Minced Garlic
1 tb
Olive Oil
2 tb
Sambal Olek
1 tb
Hosien Sauce or Oyster Sauce
1 tsp
Bean Sauce
1
Onion finely chopped
1 tsp
Minced Garlic
1 tsp
Minced Ginger
To Taste
Salt
1
Green Onion

Directions

  1. Add the olive oil to a warm pan.
  2. Sautee the onion, garlic, and ginger in the pan.
  3. Slice the tofu into cmall squares, and slowly add it to the pan.
  4. Sautee the tofu for a few minutes, until some color is added.
  5. Add the Sambal Olek, Bean Sauce, and Oyster/Hosien Sauce to the tofu.
  6. Continue sauteeing for a few minutes until well mixed.
  7. Take the tofu off the heat, and add the salt to taste.
  8. You can optionally garnish the tofu with green onion.
