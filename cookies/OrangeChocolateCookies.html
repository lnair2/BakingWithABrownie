Orange Chocolate Cookies

Chocolate and oranges are such a perfect combination, making these cookies the perfect treat. Feel free to adjust the orange extract to modify the orange flavor to your liking.
Made by Lavi
Total Time
1 hour
Servings
48 cookies

Ingredients

1 cup
Butter (unsalted/room temperature)
3 1/4 cups
All-purpose flour
1 tsp
Baking Powder
1 tsp
Salt
2 tsp
Baking Soda
1 cup
Granulated Sugar
1 cup
Light Brown Sugar
2
Large Eggs
1 tsp
Vanilla Extract
1 1/2 tsp
Orange Extract
1 tbsp
Orange Zest
2 cup
Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°, then whisk together the flour, cornstarch, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl beat together butter and the sugars until creamy. Incrementally add the eggs, combine well.
  3. In a medium bowl whisk together flour, baking powder and salt. then gradually add flour mixture, beating on low until combined.
  4. Add orange extract, vanilla extract, and orange zest, and continue beating until combined. Slowly add in the flour mixture, and then fold in chocolate chips.
  5. Grease a cookie sheet, or place down parchment paper. Then roll out 1 inch balls of dough on to the sheet. Bake for about 9-12 minutes.
  6. Remove and let cool completely on wire racks. Enjoy!
