Peanut Butter Blossoms

These are my all-time favorite cookies to make. They are quick and easy and are the perfect combination of peanut butter and chocolate. I usually like to toast these for a bit later and enjoy them warm, because the chocolate and cookies melt in your mouth.
Made by Lavi
Total Time
1 hour
Servings
36 cookies

Ingredients

1/2 cup
Butter (unsalted/room temperature)
1 1/4 cups
All-purpose flour
1/2 tsp
Baking Soda
A Pinch
Salt
1/2 cup
Granulated Sugar
1/2 cup
Light Brown Sugar
1
Large Egg
1 tsp
Vanilla Extract
1 cup
Peanut Butter
1 bag
Hershey's Chocolate Kisses

Directions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 375Â° and line baking sheet with parchment paper. Then whisk together the flour, salt, and baking soda.
  2. In a separate bowl beat the sugars with the butter until it turns into a creamy consistency. Then add the egg and vanilla and peanut butter, mixing continuously while adding.
  3. Once that mixture is well combined, slowly mix in the flour mixture. Continue mixing until the combined dough forms.
  4. Form 1 inch wide balls of dough, and set them a few inches apart.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes. If you substitute dark brown sugar, start checking on the cookies at the 9 minute mark. Depending on the color it might need to be taken out then.
  6. While they bake unwrap the kisses, because it's best to put the chocolate on top of the cookies as soon as they're out of the oven, because then the bottom of the chocolate melts into it.
  7. I think these taste best warm, so I usually let them cool for about 5-10 minutes before eating them.
