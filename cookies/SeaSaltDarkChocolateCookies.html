Sea Salt Dark Chocolate Cookies

These cookies are a copycat Levain Bakery recipe that I modified! They are very thick and not super sweet, but still yummy and also they're dairy free and gluten free!
Made by Maryam
Total Time
1 hour and 45 minutes
Servings
12-16 cookies

Ingredients

3 cups and 2 tbsp
GF All-purpose Flour
1 tsp
Baking Powder
1/4 tsp
Baking Soda
1 tsp
Kosher Salt
3/4 cup
Coconut Oil (unmelted)
3/4 cup
Light Brown Sugar
4 tsp
Dark Brown Sugar
1/2 cup
Granulated Sugar
2
Cold Eggs (lightly beaten)
1 tsp
Vanilla Extract
2 cups
Dark Chocolate Chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees, and line a large baking sheet with parchment paper. In a medium bowl, mix flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.
  2. In a standing mixer with paddle attachment, beat coconut oil for about 1 minute. Add in sugars and beat another minute until combined.
  3. Lower the speed and mix in eggs and vanilla until combined. Gradually add in flour mixture at low speed until dough forms. Finally, fold in chocolate chips with spatula.
  4. Divide the dough into 12-16 even pieces. Shape roughly into a ball but donâ€™t roll.
  5. Place on baking sheet and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before baking (the longer the better).
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes until golden brown! It is better to take these cookies out early as they will be ruined if they are overbaked! Cool for 20 minutes and enjoy!!
