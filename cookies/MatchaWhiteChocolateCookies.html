Info

Matcha lovers these cookies are addicting! I decided to add some pandan extract to these cookies to add a bit of Malaysian flavor in there, but of course that's completely optional so if you don't have that on hand you can still enjoy these cookies.
Made by Lavi
Total Time
40 minutes
Servings
24 cookies

Ingredients

2 cups
All-purpose Flour
1 tbs
Matcha Tea Powder
1/2 tsp
Baking Soda
2 Pinches
Salt
1 cup
Brown Sugar
3/4 cup
Unsalted Butter (softened)
1/4 cup
Granulated Sugar
1 1/2 tsp
Pandan Extract
1
Egg
1
Egg Yolk
1 cup
White Chocolate Chunks

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees and grease two cookie sheets.
  2. Sift the flour, baking soda, salt, and matcha powder.
  3. Combine the sugars and butter. When combined, add in the eggs and the pandan extract, and continue to mix until creamy.
  4. Slowly add in the dry ingredients, and continue to mix until a dough forms.
  5. Fold in the chopped white chocolate chunks or chips. Then spoon a tablespoon of dough on to the sheets about 2 inches apart.
  6. Bake for about 8-10 minutes and enjoy!
