Dark Chocolate Strawberry Cookies

These are my favorite cookies to make for Valentine's Day. They perfectly combine the flavors of sweet strawberries and rich dark chocolate making these cookies absolutely addicting!
Made by Lavi
Total Time
1 1/2 hour
Servings
18 cookies

Ingredients

1 stick
Butter (unsalted/room temperature)
1 1/2 cups
All-purpose flour
3/4 tsp
Baking Soda
A Pinch
Sea Salt
1 cup
Light Brown Sugar
1
Egg
1 tsp
Vanilla Extract
1 cup
Freeze Dried Strawberries
1 1/8 cup
Dark Chocolate Chips
1/8 cup
Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips
1 tsp
Coconut Oil

Directions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350Â° and grease or line baking sheet with parchment paper. Then whisk together the flour, salt, and baking soda.
  2. In a separate bowl cream the sugar and the butter. Then add the egg and vanilla, mixing continuously.
  3. Once the sugar mixture is well combined, slowly mix in the flour mixture. Continue mixing until the combined dough forms. Then fold in 1 cup of the dark chocolate chips and 1 cup of slightly crushed freeze dried strawberries.
  4. Form balls of dough, and set them a few inches apart. Then bake for 10-12 minutes or until they are slightly golden on the edges.
  5. While they bake, combine the rest of the chocolate chips and coconut oil in a microwave safe bowl. Heat the mixture in 15 second intervals, mixing in-between, until it forms a smooth consistency.

    6. *Note: I added semi-sweet chocolate chips to add a tinge of sweetness to the sauce, but depending on your preference you can do full dark chocolate or full semi-sweet!

  6. After the cookies are done baking, transfer them to a wire rack, and let them cool for a few minutes. Then drizzle the chocolate sauce over the top of them. Allow them to cool for an additional 10-15 minutes, then enjoy!
