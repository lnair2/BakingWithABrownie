Info

These are such classic cookies to make and they taste delicious. The plus is that they are super easy to make and take no effort at all. I love making these chewy ginger cookies especially for the holidays!
Made by Lavi
Total Time
40 minutes
Servings
24 cookies

Ingredients

2 1/4 cup
All-purpose Flour
3/4 cup
Unsalted Butter (softened)
1 cup
Granulated Sugar
1/4 cup
Brown Sugar
1
Egg
2 tbs
Freshly Grated Ginger
1/4 cup
Molasses (unsulphared)
1 tsp
Baking Soda
A Pinch
Salt
1/2 tsp
Cinnamon
1/2 tsp
Nutmeg

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Then in one bowl combine flour, baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt.
  2. In a second bowl beat the ginger, butter, and sugars. When the consistency is creamy and fluffy, beat in the egg and the molasses.
  3. Incrementally fold in the dry ingredients. Then chill the mixture for about an hour (super important if youâ€™re using the fresh ground ginger).
  4. Roll the dough out into 1 1/2 inch balls and place on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  5. For a chewier texture, I baked them for 15 minutes.
