Lemon Bars

These lemon bars are the perfect tart summer treat. AND they're gluten free and dairy free!!
Don't cut corners on the cooling time, because these taste the best when they're chilled.
Made by Maryam
Total Time
1 hour + 8 hours cooling
Servings
16 bars

Ingredients for Shortbread Crust

1 cup
Coconut Oil
1/2 cup
Sugar
1 tsp
Vanilla Extract
1/2 tsp
Salt
2 cups
Gluten-Free All-purpose Flour
1/4 cup
Almond Flour

Ingredients for Lemon Filling

5
Eggs
1 3/4 cups
Sugar
1 cup
Lemon Juice (about 5 lemons)
1/2 cup
Gluten-Free All-Purpose Flour
Optional
Powdered Sugar for dusting

Directions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 325Â°. Line bottom of a 9x13 glass pan with parchment paper. Make sure the paper goes over the edges so you can lift the bars out of the pan.
  2. For the crust, mix the melted coconut oil, sugar, vanilla extract, and salt.
  3. Add in the GF all-purpose flour and mix until a dough forms, it should feel greasy. Press dough into the pan evenly about 1/2 inch thick.
  4. Sprinkle almond flour over the top, and press into the top of the dough to soak up the oil as it bakes.
  5. Bake 20-30 minutes until the dough is firm and slightly browned. Remove from the oven and set aside to cool while you create the filling.
  6. For the filling, crack and whip the eggs in a large bowl.
  7. Add sugar and lemon juice (we used fresh lemons but lemon juice works also). If you are freshly squeezing, make sure no seeds get into the juice.
  8. Stir in the GF flour to thicken the paste. Pour the filling on top of the shortbread crust and bake for 25-27 minutes or until the filling no longer jiggles.
  9. Cover the bars and chill for 2 hours in the fridge before cutting.
  10. After cutting into bars, chill overnight before serving.
  11. You can sprinkle the powdered sugar over the bars right before serving as the sugar will dissolve into the bars over time. Enjoy!
