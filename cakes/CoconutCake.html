Info

Lavi made this cake for Mother's Day! The cake was really moist and the icing was super fluffy, we almost finished the entire cake in one sitting.
Total Time
1 hour and 40 minutes
Servings
16
Calories per serving
478

Ingredients for the Cake

1/2 cup
Butter (salted/room temperature)
2 cups
All-purpose flour
1 tbsp
Baking Powder
1/4 tsp
Salt
1 1/2 cups
Granulated Sugar
3
Large Eggs
1 cup
Milk (room temperature)
2 tsp
Vanilla

Ingredients for the Icing

1/2 cup
Butter (room temperature)
3 cups
Powdered Sugar
2 tsp
Vanilla
3 cups
Sweetened Shredded Coconut

Directions for Cake

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350Â°, and lightly grease with butter and flour two cake pans
  2. In a medium bowl at medium speed beat together butter and sugar for 2 minutes until combined then add eggs and beat, combine well.
  3. In another bowl whisk together flour, baking powder and salt. Then gradually add flour mixture, beating on low until combined.
  4. Add milk and vanilla and continue beating until combined.
  5. Divide batter into prepared pans. Bake until golden (use toothpick to test for doneness), approximately 30-35 minutes.
  6. Remove and let cool completely on wire racks.

Directions for Icing

  1. In a medium bowl beat together butter and powdered sugar and vanilla on medium-high speed until creamy approximately 2 minutes.
  2. Slowly fold in coconut flakes.
  3. To frost the cake, place the first layer on a large plate top side down, spread with 1/2 of the frosting, place the second cake top side down and frost with remaining icing.
  4. We are crazy coconut fans and covered the top entirely with coconut flakes, but that of course is optional. Enjoy!
