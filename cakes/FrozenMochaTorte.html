Total Time
9 hours (including cooling time)
Servings
16

Ingredients

2 boxes
Chocolate Wafers or Cookies
1/4 cup
Sugar
1/4 cup
Butter (unsalted, melted)
8 oz
Cream Cheese
14 oz
Sweetened Condensed Milk
2/3 cup
Chocolate Sauce
1 cup
Heavy Cream
1
Chocolate Bar
2 tbsp
Instant Coffee
1 cup
Heavy Cream (whipped)
Garnish
Cocoa Powder

Directions

  1. In a food processor, combine wafers/cookies, sugar and melted butter, and grind until finely crushed.
  2. Press onto bottom of greased 9 inch springform pan to form bottom crust. Set aside.
  3. To make the chocolate sauce use a small pan and over low heat start to melt the chocolate bar.
  4. Slowly add in heavy cream while continually stirring to help create a sauce. Note: the full cup of heavy cream might not be necessary, create a darker color sauce for a rich chocolate flavor or a lighter sauce for more of the coffee flavor to peak through.
  5. In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese, milk, and chocolate sauce until smooth.
  6. Dissolve instant coffee in 1/2 cup of hot water and add to mixture. Fold in whipped cream until combined.
  7. Pour mixture over crust and cover with cling wrap. Note: have the cling wrap lightly touch the top of mixture to avoid freezer burn. Put in freezer for 8 hours or overnight.
  8. Remove from freezer 10 minutes before serving and light run a knife around edge of pan to help remove. Garnish with a dusting of cocoa powder and enjoy!
