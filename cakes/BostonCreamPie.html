Info

I made this cake for Parent's Anniversaty. This one was a little conplicated because the filling had to be the right consistency in order to create the layer in between the two cakes.
Total Time
1 hour and 45 minutes
Servings
16
Calories per serving
Approximately 340 calories

Ingredients for the Cake

3 1/4 cups
Butter (unsalted/room temperature)
2 1/2 cups
All-purpose flour
3 1/2 tsp
Baking Powder
1/2 tsp
Salt
3 tbsp
Vegetable Oil
1 1/2 cups
Granulated Sugar
4
Large Eggs
1 1/4 cups
Milk (room temperature)
1 tbsp
Vanilla

Ingredients for the Custard

4
Egg Yolks
3/4 cup
Sugar (Granulated)
2 tsp
Vanilla
2 cups
Milk
2 tbsp
Butter (salted and at room temperature)
4 tbsp
Cornstarch

Ingredients for the Chocolate Ganache

1 1/4 cup
Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips or Chopped Chocoalte Chunks
2 tbsp
Corn Syrup
3/4 cup
Heavy Whipping Cream

Directions for the Custard

  1. To make the pastry cream, put the egg yolks in a medium sized bowl and gently beat them together.
  2. Add the sugar, cornstarch and milk to a large saucepan and mix until smooth, make sure to really combine them to avoid clumping in the heat.
  3. Cook, stirring continuously, over medium heat until mixture begins to really thicken. Simmer for 2 minutes, then remove from heat.
  4. Remove from heat and add the butter and vanilla extract. Stir until smooth, then pour into a bowl and set in the fridge to cool completely.

Directions for the Ganache

  1. To make the chocolate ganache, add the chocolate chips and corn syrup to a medium bowl.
  2. Heat the heavy whipping cream just until it begins to boil, then pour over the chocolate chips and corn syrup and whisk until smooth.

Directions for the Cake

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350Â°, and lightly grease with butter and flour two cake pans
  2. In a medium bowl combine the flour, baking powder and salt in a medium sized bowl and set aside.
  3. Add the butter, sugar and oil to and large mixer bowl and beat together until light in color and the consistency is creamy and fluffy.
  4. Add the eggs and vanilla extract and mix until completely integrated and smooth.
  5. Add half of the dry ingredients to the batter and mix until combined.
  6. Add the milk and mix until well combined.
  7. Add the remaining dry ingredients and mix until smooth.
  8. Divide the batter evenly between the cakes pans and bake for 22-25 minutes, or until top is slightly golden.
  9. Place first cake layer on a serving platter, then top with the pastry cream. Then add the remaining layer directly on top of the cake.
  10. Pour the ganache on top of the cake and spread over the top and sides, or allow to drip over the edges.
