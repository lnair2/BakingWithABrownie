I changed it up for Thanksgiving this year, and instead of making pumpkin pie, I decided to make this pumpkin cream cheese roll cake. Make sure to immediately roll the cake in the towel as soon as it's out of the oven to ensure it stays in the shape and doesn't crack.
Total Time
2 hour and 40 minutes
Servings
16

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees and line a jelly roll pan with parchment paper. Grease and flour the pan.
  2. Combine the flour, salt, baking powder, cinnamon, and ginger.
  3. In another bowl beat the eggs and sugar until fluffy. Mix in the pumpkin and vanilla and then slowly add in the dry ingredients.
  4. Pour the batter onto the pan and spread into an even layer. Then bake for 15 minutes.
  5. Line a dish towel with powdered sugar, and immediately transfer the cake onto the towel. Roll the cake from one short end to the other and place in the fridge to cool.
  6. For the filling beat the powdered sugar, butter, cream cheese, and vanilla. In a small glass mix in the water with the espresso powder. Add the coffee mixture to the filling to your taste, and continue mixing until light and fluffy.
  7. After chilling, unroll the cake and spread the filling on top of it and immediately re-roll the cake and refrigerate for a couple of hours. Dust with powdered sugar or top with chocolate before serving.
