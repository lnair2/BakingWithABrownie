Flourless Chocolate Cake

I love this gluten-free and dairy-free flourless chocolate cake! This cake had such a rich chocolatey flavor that I couldn't get enough of.
Made by Maryam
Total Time
2 hours and 30 minutes (including cooling time)
Servings
16

Ingredients for the Cake

10 oz
Chocolate Chips (1/2 bittersweet and 1/2 semi-sweet)
1 cup
Vegan Butter (Earth Balance)
3/4 cups
Sugar
3/4 cup
Dark Brown Sugar
1/2 tsp
Salt
6
Eggs
2 tsp
Vanilla Extract
1/4 tsp
Almond Milk
1 cup
Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
Optional
Berries
Optional
Powdered Sugar

Directions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350Â°, and grease a 9-inch springform pan and line the bottom of the pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a saucepan on low heat, melt the butter and chocolate chips stirring constantly until combined.
  3. Add in both the sugars, salt, vanilla extract, and almond milk. Continue heating for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat and pour into a large glass bowl. Add in the eggs and beat until smooth. Then add cocoa powder and continue mixing until combined.
  5. Pour batter into the pan and bake for 30-35 minutes or until the center is set when gently shaken.
  6. Remove and let cool on wire rack for about 20 minutes. Then move to the fridge for to cool for an additional 10 minutes.
  7. The cake will be chilled enough to remove the outer ring of the pan and prepare for decorating.
  8. For the topping we dusted with powdered sugar and then decorated with strawberries and raspberries.
  9. Refrigerate for an additonal hour before serving. Enjoy!
