Total Time
2 hours
Servings
16

Ingredients for Cake

2 cups
All-purpose Flour
1/4 cup
Granulated Sugar
1 tsp
Baking Soda
1/2 tsp
Salt
1 tbsp
Ground Ginger
1 1/2 tsp
Cinnamon
1/4 tsp
Allspice
1/4 tsp
Ground Cloves
1/2 cup
Unsalted Vegan Butter (melted)
3/4 cup
Molasses
2
Eggs
1 cup
Buttermilk
2 tsp
Vanilla Extract

Ingredients for Icing

1/2 cup
Vegan Butter
1 1/2 cups
Powdered Sugar
2 tsp
Ground Ginger
1/2 tsp
Cinnamon
Optional
Sprinkles

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees and grease a 9x5 bread tin.
  2. Start by mixing all dry ingredients in a large bowl (flour, sugar, baking soda, salt, ginger, cinnamon, allspice and cloves).
  3. Next, in a separate bowl mix melted butter and molasses until combined. Once combined, add eggs and vanilla extract and beat.
  4. Once eggs are incorporated, add buttermilk and stir until mixture is smooth. If you don't have buttermilk you can make it by by mixing 1 cup of almond milk with 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar, let sit in the fridge for 4 minutes.
  5. Slowly add wet ingredients to dry ingredients mixing well until combined. (Be careful to not over mix.) Pour batter into pan and bake for 50-55 minutes. You will know when the bread is done when you can stick a toothpick in the middle and it comes out clean.
  6. Allow loaf to cool. While cooling prepare icing by beating the vegan butter until smooth. Once smooth add in powdered sugar and spices until incorporated. Decorate and enjoy!
