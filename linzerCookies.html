Info

These cookies were so addicting! They were amazingly buttery and tart and I really wish I made way more of these. I love these because you can easily swap the jam flavors for something that you want.
Total Time
2 hours
Servings
24 cookies
Calories per serving
212

Ingredients

3 cups
All-purpose Flour
1 1/2 cups
Butter (unsalted room temperature)
3 tb
Water
1/4 tsp
Salt
1 tsp
Vanilla Extract
1 cup
Jam (whatever kind you want, I used strawberry and blueberry)
Optional
Powdered Sugar for dusting

Directions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350Â°, and line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl mix together the butter, sugar, and vanilla until well combined.
  3. Sift in the flour, and add in the water and salt until it combines into dough.
  4. Cover the dough with plastic wrap and place in the fridge for 45 minutes.
  5. Roll the dough out into a 1/4 inch sheet, if it is still sticking dust some flour over the top.
  6. Cut our shapes for all of your cookies, and using a smaller circle cutter, cut holes in half of them (these will be the tops).
  7. Place the cookies onto the sheets and chill for an additional 20 mintues.
  8. Bake for 20 minutes or until edges are golden.
  9. Cook the cookies on a wire rack.
  10. When cooled, spread jam on the bottom cookies and sandwich with the tops. You can dust the tops with powdered sugar as well.
