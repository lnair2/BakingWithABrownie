Info

This is one of the best summer desserts ever, each bite tastes fresh and fluffy. It literally takes 30 minutes to make and assemble it too, the hardest part is patiently waiting for it to chill. Plus the colors of the berries make it the perfect July 4th dessert!
Total Time
30 minutes + 6 hours cooling
Servings
10

Ingredients

1 cup
Seedless Berry Jam (I used 1/2 raspberry and 1/2 strawberry)
1 1/2 pouds
Strawberries (hulled and sliced)
1 quart/dt>
Raspberries
1 quart
Blueberries
1 1/2 cups
Heavy Whipping Cream
16 oz
Cream Cheese
1 3/4 cups
Powdered Sugar
1 tsp
Vanilla Extract
2 packages
Lady Fingers Biscuits

Directions

  1. Set aside some berries for the topping.
  2. Microwave the jams in a medium bowl for about 2 minutes. Add the fresh berries and gently toss to make sure it's evenly distributed and coated.
  3. Whisk the heavy cream until fluffy stiff peaks form, and set aside.
  4. In a large bowl beat the cream cheese untl creamy, then add the powdered sugar. Beat in the vanilla, and then incrementally fold in the the whipped cream until combined.
  5. For assembly, cover the bottom of the trifle dish with a layer of lady fingers biscuits, followed by 1/3 of the berry mixture, then 1/3 of the cream.
  6. Continue the above process, ending with the cream mixture on the top.
  7. Garnish with the remainder of the berries, and then chill in the fridge for at least 6 hours or overnight before serving. Enjoy!
