Info

These are super rich brownies and they are very easy to make! They make such a yummy vegan treat!
Made by Maryam
Total Time
1 hour
Servings
12 bars

Ingredients

1 1/2 cups
Dark Chocolate Chips (I used Nestle Tollhouse allergen-free)
1/4 cup + 2 tbsp
Coconut Oil
2 tbsp
Water
3 tbsp
Vanilla Yogurt (I used So Delicious vegan brand)
1/2 tsp
Salt
1/2 tsp
Baking Soda
1/2 cup
Dark Brown Sugar
3/4 cup
Flour

Ingredients for Ganache (optional)

3 tbsp
Almond Milk
1/2 cup
Dark Chocolate Chips
2-4 tbsp
Powdered Sugar

Directions

  1. Use the coconut oil to grease a 8x8 brownie pan and preheat your oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Start with 1 cup of dark chocolate in a medium size mixing bowl. Melt your coconut oil and pour over the chocolate to melt.
  3. Once melted, add in yogurt, salt, baking soda and dark brown sugar. Combine all ingredients and fold in the flour. Add the remaining 1/2 cup of chocolate chips and fold into mixture.
  4. The mixture will be very thick so smooth it with a spoon or spatula into your pan and bake for 28-30 minutes. (Because these are fudge brownies itâ€™s better to under bake)!
  5. Let your brownies cool in the fridge and prepare a chocolate ganache topping if desired! Best to enjoy these cold!

    6. Directions for Ganache

  6. For optional ganache, melt chocolate with almond milk in the microwave for 1 minute in 30 second increments stirring consistently.
  7. Once melted, slowly mix in powdered sugar to thicken and then pour over the brownies!
  8. Allow this to cool in the fridge before serving.
