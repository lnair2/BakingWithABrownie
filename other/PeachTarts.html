Info

These are the perfect summer tart!
Made by Maryam
Servings
6-8 tarts

Ingredients for Crust

3 1/3 cups
All-purpose Flour
1/4 cup
Sugar
1/2 tsp
Salt
1 cup
Unsalted Butter (cold)
1/2 cup and 2 tbsp
Cold Water
2
Egg Yolks

Ingredients for Filling

6-8
Fresh Ripe Peaches (skinned and sliced)
2/3 cup
Sugar
2 tbsp
Cornstarch
Pinch
Salt
1 cup
Water
1 tsp
Lemon Juice

Directions

  1. Start with the crust! Mix flour, sugar, and salt in a large bowl. Next cut in the butter until the crust resembles small crumbles.
  2. Add in water and egg yolks and stir/knead until dough is formed.
  3. Split dough into 8 balls and press into the bottom of 4 inch tart pans. (Note if you canâ€™t find tart pans your can also use small pie crust pans and cut off the excess crust.)
  4. Bake at 400 degrees for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.
  5. For the filling, in a medium saucepan combine sugar, cornstarch, and salt.
  6. Continue to cook for 2-4 minutes until mixture thickens. Remove from heat and stir in peaches (I used a mixture of white and golden peaches for variety) and lemon juice.
  7. Fill into tart shells and chill until ready to serve!
